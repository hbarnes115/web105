In Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition, the Rogue is a nimble
and stealthy character class. Rogues are known for their
cunning and dexterity, making them exceptional thieves and assassins.
They excel in stealth, lock-picking, acrobatics, and disarming traps,
which are essential skills for covert operations. Their signature ability is the
"Sneak Attack," allowing them to deal extra damage when they strike their
foes unexpectedly. Rogues can choose various specializations, such as the
Thief, Assassin, or Arcane Trickster, each adding unique abilities and
playstyles. They are the masters of subterfuge and surprise, making them
invaluable members of any intrepid adventuring party.
