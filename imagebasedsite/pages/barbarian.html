In Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition, the Barbarian is a class that embodies
untamed fury and primal power. These formidable warriors channel their inner rage
to become relentless combatants. When in a rage, they gain increased strength and resilience,
excelling in melee combat and withstanding attacks that would defeat others.
Barbarians are known for their unbridled combat prowess and fearlessness in the face of danger.
They often have unique features and abilities tied to their primal instincts,
making them the epitome of raw physical might and ferocity on the battlefield, making them a force to be reckoned with.
