In Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition, the Sorcerer is a unique spellcasting class
with innate magical abilities. Sorcerers derive their power from their bloodline,
ancestry, or some other intrinsic source, setting them apart from other spellcasters.
They cast spells using charisma as their primary attribute and possess limited spells known,
but their unique feature is "Metamagic."
