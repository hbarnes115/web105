In Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition, the Paladin is a class of holy knights,
sworn to uphold justice and protect the innocent. These devoted warriors
wield divine magic, often granted by a deity, and follow a strict moral code.
Paladins are formidable melee combatants, able to smite evil with
righteous fury and heal the wounded with their magic. They may also have a sacred mount,
such as a warhorse. Each Paladin's abilities are deeply tied to their chosen Oath,
which determines their special powers and the nature of their divine commitment.
They are unwavering champions of faith, embodying the ideals of righteousness and virtue
in their quests.
