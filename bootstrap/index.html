Paladins by Bootstrap

Paladins

  • holy warriors in service to an oath
  • Have magical power granted by the divine
  • Have abilities unique to their class
  • Are usually aligned "Lawful Good"
  • Can sometimes be "Chaotic Good"

Paldin Oaths

Various Paladin Oaths come with different values

Oath Values Rating
Oath of Devotion Justice, virtue, order 9/10
Oath of the Ancients Good above law and chaos 5/10
Oath of Vengance Punish the sinful 7/10
