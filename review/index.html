On the Edge of the Dark Sea of Darkness

Andrew Peterson

On the Edge of the Dark Sea of Darkness is a fantasy adventure
following the three Igiby chilren: Janner, Kalmar, and Leeli, as they
discover the truth about who they are and embark on an adventure. This
book is the first in the Wingfeather Saga series. Written by folk singer
and songwriter Andrew Peterson, the book has been adapted
into an animated TV show.

Janner

Janner is the oldest Igiby child. An avid reader, he longs for adventure.
He is conflicted between a need to protect his siblings
and a want to be free of responsibility.

Kalmar

Kalmar is the middle Igiby child. An artist and athlete, Kalmar is plagued
by a fear of heights.

Leeli

Leeli is the youngest Igiby. She is a talented musician, and does her best
to keep up with her brothers despite her limp and crutch

The Adventures Begin

One day, the children encounter the sea dragons as they make their annual visit to their town.

The dragons had fallen silent.They had halted their dance and were gazing up at the cliffs.
Though they were leagues away and the dusk made it difficult to see,
Janner knew with a shudder that the sea dragons were watching them.They were listening.

Andrew Peterson, On the Edge of the Dark Sea of Darkness
