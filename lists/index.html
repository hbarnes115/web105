How to Make a Vesper Martini

  1. I. Gather Your Ingredients
    1. Gin
    2. Vodka
    3. Lillet Blanc (a French aperitif wine)
    4. Lemon twist or peel for garnish
    5. Ice cubes
  2. II. Prepare Your Equipment
    1. Martini glass
    2. Mixing glass or cocktail shaker
    3. Jigger (for measuring)
  3. III. Measure the Spirits
    1. 3 oz of gin
    2. 1 oz of vodka
    3. 1/2 oz of Lillet Blanc
  4. IV. Add Ice
    1. Fill the mixing glass or shaker with ice cubes.
  5. V. Stir or Shake
    1. Stir the ingredients gently with a bar spoon for about 15-20 seconds if you prefer a smooth and clear martini.
    2. Alternatively, shake the mixture with a cocktail shaker for about 10-15 seconds if you want a slightly cloudy appearance.
  6. VI. Strain
    1. Strain the martini into a chilled martini glass.
  7. VII. Garnish
    1. Take a lemon twist or peel and express the oils over the martini by holding it over the glass and giving it a gentle twist. Run it around the rim for extra aroma.
    2. Optionally, you can drop the twist into the martini for added flavor.
  8. VIII. Serve
    1. Serve your Vesper Martini immediately while it's still cold.
  9. IX. Enjoy Responsibly
    1. Sip and savor your Vesper Martini responsibly. It's a classic cocktail with a sophisticated flavor.
